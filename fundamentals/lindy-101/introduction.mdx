## Welcome to Lindy

Lindy is an AI agent platform that helps you automate complex workflows by connecting AI agents to your tools and data. Think of Lindy as your AI teammate that can handle tasks across your entire tech stack.

## What Can You Build?

Lindy excels at workflows that require:
- **Email automation and response management** - Intelligent email triage, auto-responses, and follow-ups
- **Sales lead qualification and CRM updates** - Automated lead scoring, qualification, and CRM data enrichment
- **Meeting scheduling and coordination** - Smart calendar management, availability coordination, and meeting prep
- **Customer support automation** - Ticket routing, FAQ responses, and escalation management
- **Social media management** - Content scheduling, engagement monitoring, and response automation
- **Multi-channel outreach** - Coordinated campaigns across phone calls, text, email, and social media
