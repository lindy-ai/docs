## Overview

People Data Labs provides professional data enrichment for individuals and companies, enabling you to enhance profiles, improve lead scoring, and personalize outreach.

## Available Actions

### Person Enrichment

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Enrich A Person">
    Enrich person profiles with additional data using identifiable information.
    
    Input: Email, name, phone, or LinkedIn URL
    
    Returns: Full profile including job title, company, social profiles, and more
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Search for People">
    Find people by broad criteria like:
    - "data scientists in San Francisco"
    - "people with @company.com email"
    
    Note: Avoid using to find specific person. Default to 5 results unless specified otherwise.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Find Person by Social Network">
    Find a person by a social network URL. A social network URL may match multiple people. The most relevant people are returned first.
    
    Input: LinkedIn, Twitter, or other social profile URLs
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Find Person By Phone">
    Find a person by phone number.
    
    Example: +1 555-234-1234
    
    Note: A phone number exactly identifies one person.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Company Enrichment

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Enrich A Company">
    Retrieve detailed company information using various identifiers and parameters.
    
    Input: Company name, domain, or other identifiers
    
    Returns: Company size, industry, location, and more
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Search for Companies">
    Find companies by broad criteria like:
    - "tech startups in San Francisco"
    - "companies with over 1000 employees"
    
    Note: Avoid using to find specific company. Default to 5 results unless specified otherwise.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

- Use specific identifiers (email, phone) for individual lookups
- Use broad criteria for discovering new prospects
- Combine person and company data for complete profiles
- Respect data privacy regulations when using enriched data

<Tip>
People Data Labs actions work great with CRM integrations, email automation, and sales workflows to create data-driven processes.
</Tip>
