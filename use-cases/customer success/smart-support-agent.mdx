## Quick Overview

With the Smart Support flow, Lindy will:

- **Monitor** a support inbox (Gmail, Outlook—or swap in Help Desk APIs)  
- **Detect** whether the message needs a knowledge-base reply or human help  
- **Search** your docs, FAQs, and policies for accurate answers  
- **Respond** in your brand voice or draft a reply for approval  
- **Log** tickets and escalations to Slack, Zendesk, or HubSpot  

You'll find the **Email Responder** template in your Lindy dashboard to kick-start the flow.

## Setting Up Your Smart Support Agent

### Step 1: Connect the Trigger

<Frame>
  <img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Gmail trigger" />
</Frame>

1. Select `Gmail – Email Received` (swap for `Zendesk – Ticket Created` later).  
2. Add optional filters—for example, skip internal or marketing emails.

### Step 2: Determine If a Reply Is Needed

<Frame>
  <img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Requires reply condition" />
</Frame>

`Condition` prompt:

```
Only continue if the sender asks a question about products, orders, or policies.
```

### Step 3: Search the Knowledge Base

<Frame>
  <img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Knowledge base search" />
</Frame>

- Action: `Search Knowledge Base`  
- Attach FAQs, shipping policies, refund rules, and troubleshooting docs.

### Step 4: Verify an Answer Exists

<Frame>
  <img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Found answer condition" />
</Frame>

- If answer found → continue.  
- If not found → `Slack – DM` the thread to Support queue.

### Step 5: Draft or Send the Reply

<Frame>
  <img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Send reply" />
</Frame>

Body prompt:

```
Respond with the KB answer in a friendly, supportive tone (≤100 words).
```

Toggle `Ask for Confirmation` to review drafts during week 1.

### Step 6: Update the Ticket System (Optional)

<Frame>
  <img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Ticket update" />
</Frame>

- Action: `Zendesk – Update Ticket` or `HubSpot – Add Note` with the reply and status.

## Advanced Customizations

| Trick                  | What it does                                                                    |
|------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Multi-Channel Support  | Swap Gmail for Zendesk, Intercom, or Slack slash-commands.                      |
| Sentiment Escalation   | Flag angry tone → route to human immediately.                                   |
| SLA Timer              | `Wait` + Condition → alert if no answer within 2 h during business hours.       |
| Auto-Survey            | Send CSAT form 24 h after ticket closure.                                       |

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep KB Fresh">
    Review policy and product docs weekly; outdated articles create wrong answers.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Short & Clear">
    Aim for one-screen replies; link out for deeper resources.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Human First">
    Escalate order issues or refund disputes early to avoid churn.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Channel Flexibility">
    The same flow works for chat or SMS—just swap the trigger/action pair.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
