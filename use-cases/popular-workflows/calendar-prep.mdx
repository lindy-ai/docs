## Quick Overview

Connect Lindy to your calendar and let it:

- **Trigger** before each meeting to gather context  
- **Research** attendees across LinkedIn and the web  
- **Summarize** past emails, CRM notes, and action items  
- **Draft** a tailored brief or agenda for the call  
- **Deliver** everything via Slack, email, or Docs  

You'll find the **Meeting Prep** template in your Lindy dashboard to kick‑start the flow.

## Setting Up Your Meeting Prep Flow

### Step&nbsp;1: Daily Trigger & Calendar Pull

<Frame>
  <img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Time trigger and calendar query" />
</Frame>

1. Choose `Scheduler – Daily at 6 AM` (or any offset you prefer).  
2. Add `Google Calendar – List Events` for the next business day.  

### Step&nbsp;2: Loop Through Events

<Frame>
  <img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Loop over events" />
</Frame>

- Insert `Loop – For Each` to iterate over every upcoming event.  
- Inside the loop, reference event fields such as `attendees`, `title`, and `hangoutLink`.

### Step&nbsp;3: Enrich Attendees

<Frame>
  <img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Attendee research" />
</Frame>

For each attendee:

1. `AI Agent – Search the Internet`  

Find the LinkedIn URL for `attendee.name` at `attendee.company`.

2. `People Data Labs – Enrich Person` to pull title, phone, and company info.

### Step&nbsp;4: Pull Past Communications

<Frame>
<img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Search past emails" />
</Frame>

- Action: `Gmail – Search Emails`  
- Query:

from:`attendee.email` OR to:`attendee.email` newer_than:365d

Store snippets or thread links for context.

### Step 5: Draft the Brief

<Frame>
<img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Generate meeting brief" />
</Frame>

Use `AI Agent – Generate Text`:

Write a concise prep brief:
- Name, role, LinkedIn
- Recent news or funding
- Last email summary
- Open action items
- Limit to 150 words.


### Step 6: Send or Save

<Frame>
  <img src="/lindy-brand-assets/placeholder.png" alt="Send prep summary" />
</Frame>

- Option A: `Slack – Send DM` with the compiled briefs.  
- Option B: `Gmail – Send Email` to yourself or the team.  
- Option C: `Google Docs – Append` to a rolling prep doc.

## Advanced Customizations

| Trick                     | What it does                                                                  |
|---------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Agenda Drafting           | Have Lindy propose a 3‑point agenda and email it to attendees pre‑call.        |
| CRM Insight Pull          | Fetch lead/opportunity fields from HubSpot or Salesforce and add to the brief. |
| Company News Monitor      | Use Perplexity to scan for funding, hires, or press releases within 30 days.    |
| Last‑Call Notes Injection | Search past meeting transcripts and surface key decisions or blockers.         |

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Respect Attendee Privacy">
    Share briefs internally only; avoid forwarding personal data to external guests.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Keep Research Timely">
    Limit web news searches to the past 90 days for relevance.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Stay Concise">
    Aim for one screen‑length briefs—signal over noise.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Automate, Then Review">
    Skim each brief the first week to fine‑tune prompts before full auto‑send.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
