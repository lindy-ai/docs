## Quick Overview

Each week, Lindy will:

- **Email** every employee a 3-question pulse check  
- **Listen** for their replies and add them to a Google Sheet  
- **Summarize** themes, sentiment, and standout quotes every Friday  
- **Send** a concise digest to leadership via Slack or email

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep It Short">
    Three questions = highest response rates; avoid survey fatigue.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Reinforce Anonymity">
    Strip names/emails when appending rows so answers stay candid.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Close the Loop">
    Start Monday stand-up by highlighting one change inspired by the feedback.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Iterate on Prompts">
    Tweak wording after the first two digests to match team voice and length.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
